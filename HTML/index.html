About Us

Consultancy

CONCYBER is an actively developing company specializing in providing services in the field of cybersecurity, search and analysis of information in open, commercial registries, as well as social networks, closed resources using Ethical Hacking and OSINT methods.
CONCYBER experts have many years of experience in cyberspace. We offer various options for technical solutions both to prevent hostile actions in relation to the technical infrastructure of the company and to minimize possible negative consequences. We are developing a set of measures to increase the privacy of business owners, as well as employees of companies and individuals, in the conditions of modern transparency of personal life.

Objective

One of the directions of CONCYBER's work is focused on supporting businesses when working with counterparties by qualitatively monitoring potential partners and identifying possible risks associated with them. We facilitate the entry into new sales markets, including certain countries in Africa, the CIS, the Middle East and Europe, by searching and verifying potential partners.
